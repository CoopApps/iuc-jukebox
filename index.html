About IUC Jukebox

INNOCENT UNTIL CAUGHT — Original soundtrack player.

The music in Innocent Until Caught was composed for the Roland MT-32 sound module, a LA (Linear Arithmetic) synthesis synthesiser released in 1987. The MT-32 uses a combination of PCM samples and digitally-controlled analogue-style oscillators to produce its characteristic sound.

These tracks were captured via MT-32 emulation using Munt, an open-source MT-32 emulator, with the original ROM set. The recordings faithfully reproduce the timbre and reverb characteristics of the hardware unit.

Files are in Ogg Vorbis format. Place your .ogg files in the audio/ subdirectory alongside this page.
